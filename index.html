Most consulting firms separate advisory from research. PrairiePulse does not. Every engagement draws on both: structured methodologies from the research discipline, applied to real organisational and policy challenges.

We are not a market research agency. We are not a generalist consulting firm. We operate at the intersection — where evidence informs strategy and strategy defines inquiry.

Our engagements are scoped, time-bound, and tied to a specific outcome. Global in outlook. Disciplined in execution.

How We Work

01

Define

One Agreed Objective

Every engagement begins with a single, agreed objective. No ambiguity in scope. No moving targets mid-engagement. Clarity is established before any work begins.

02

Structure

Discrete Task Units

Work is divided into defined units, each with explicit inputs, outputs, and timelines. Progress is transparent at every stage. No black boxes.

03

Deliver

Actionable Outputs

Outcomes are documented, measurable, and transferable. Clients receive outputs they can act on directly — not presentations that require interpretation.

Engagements

We do not offer service packages. We structure engagements around your objective.

Each mandate is scoped to a single, defined challenge — strategic, operational, research, or advisory. Scope, methodology, and deliverables are agreed in writing before work begins.

Consulting Advisory

How an Engagement Begins

Step one

Submit a Brief

Use the contact form below to describe the objective you are working toward. A summary of two or three sentences is sufficient to begin.

Step two

Fit Assessment

We assess scope feasibility and engagement fit within two business days. If there is alignment, we respond directly — no automated sequences.

Step three

Engagement Proposal

We propose a structured engagement with defined outputs, timeline, and terms. Nothing proceeds until scope and objective are agreed in writing.

Step four

Work Begins

With scope confirmed, work begins against the agreed task units. Progress is reported at each defined milestone. No surprises.
